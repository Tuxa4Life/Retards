Set in Kutaisi, 1.1 km from Colchis Fountain, Happy Stay Hotel features rooms with garden views and free WiFi. This 3-star hotel offers a tour desk. The accommodation offers airport transfers, while a car rental service is also available. At the hotel every room includes air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kitchen, a dining area and a private bathroom with slippers, a shower and a hairdryer. At Happy Stay Hotel rooms include bed linen and towels. White Bridge is 2.9 km from the accommodation, while Bagrati Cathedral is 3.5 km away. The nearest airport is Kutaisi International Airport, 21 km from Happy Stay Hotel. Couples particularly like the location
