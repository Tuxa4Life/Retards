Located in Tskaltubo, 7 km from Prometheus Cave, Villa Edemi provides accommodation with a garden, free private parking and a terrace. At Villa Edemi each room comes with bed linen and towels. A continental breakfast is available daily at the accommodation. Guests at Villa Edemi will be able to enjoy activities in and around Tskaltubo, like fishing. The nearest airport is Kutaisi International, 20 km from the inn, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
