Situated 2.9 km from Grigoleti Beach, Aparthotel Alfa features accommodation with a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, a garden and a 24-hour front desk for your convenience. The apartment provides guests with a balcony, pool views, a seating area, cable flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge and an oven, and a private bathroom with shower and slippers. For added convenience, the property can provide towels and bed linen for an extra charge. Aparthotel Alfa provides a children's playground and a sun terrace. The nearest airport is Batumi International Airport, 45 km from the accommodation.
