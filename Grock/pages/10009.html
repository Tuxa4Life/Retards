Shanshe in Gurjaani provides accommodation with a bar, a garden and a terrace. The air-conditioned rooms provide a garden view and come with a desk and free WiFi. All units at the guest house are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Every room is fitted with a private bathroom with a bidet, bathrobes and slippers. All rooms include a wardrobe.
